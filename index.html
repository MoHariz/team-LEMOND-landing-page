Age Friendly Business

"Learn, Self-Reflect, and Change your business’ work environment for all ages."

Team LEMOND

Our Mission

With our passion for social impact, our mission is to empower older, more experienced workers and promote age-friendly practices so they can provide age-friendly customer service and become age-friendly employers.
Team LEMOND

Project Overview

Problem Statement

With a larger number of Seattle’s population becoming older, we noticed that older people are sometimes:
  • At a disadvantage when trying to find work opportunities.
  • Not receiving reasonable accomodations in the workforce.
  • Not getting the best customer service.

Target User Groups/Audience

Business owners/ Manager

Desire:
Seeks to provide positive experiences for customers and employees of all ages.

Pain Points:
Not aware/do not apply Age-Friendly practices to their business.
City of Seattle

Desire:
Seeks to make the community a more age-friendly environment.

Pain point:
Don’t have the manpower and funds to establish a city/system that can record all the information.

Our Solution

In response to this existing problem, we are creating a business tool website that calculates the age-friendly scores for a business so that business and their managers can recognize what it means to provide age-friendly customer service and to be an age-friendly employer.
Team LEMOND

Project Features

With a larger number of Seattle’s population becoming older, we are here to create a business tool that helps businesses and organization recognize what it means to be an age-friendly employer to make it a more age-friendly work environment for all.

Related resources for business owners to have a deep understanding about age-friendly.

Online assessment testing to get a score about company and review the tips about how to improve.

Dashboard to track the companies who took the tests and let sponsor know their next step to improve.
