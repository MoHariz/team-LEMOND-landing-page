Age Friendly Business

Learn, Self-Reflect, and Change your business’ work environment for all ages using our website.

Our Mission

With our passion for social impact, our mission is to empower older, more experienced workers and promote age-friendly practices so they can provide age-friendly customer service and become age-friendly employers.

Our Impact

With a larger number of Seattle’s population becoming older, we are here to create a business tool that helps businesses and organization recognize what it means to be an age-friendly employer to make it a more age-friendly work environment for all.
